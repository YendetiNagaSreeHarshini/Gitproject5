NOISE POLLUTION


Noise pollution is the disturbing or excessive noise that may harm the activity or balance of human or animal life. The source of most outdoor noise worldwide is mainly caused by machines and transportation systems,motor vehicles, aircraft and trains.Outdor noise is summarized by the world environmental noise.Poor urban planning may give rise to noise pollution, since side-by-side industrial and residential buildings can result in noise pollution in the residential areas.
Noise pollution affects both health and behaviour. Unwanted sound (noise) can damage psychological health. Noise pollution can cause trouble, hypertension, high stress levels,tinnitus,hearing loss,sleep disturbances and other harmfull effects.

Causes of Noise Pollution:

1.Public transportation
2.Noise pollution from cars Construction Children Events
3.Domestic appliances Agriculture Alarm systems Military Storms Rainfalls Thunder Urban planning Insufficient urban planning can be regarded as one main cause of noise pollution. Especially in poor countries, people often live in crowded areas in which they will be surrounded by an excessive amount of noise all the time.
